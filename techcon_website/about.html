Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and developers. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated technology conferences in the tech industry.

Today, TechCon continues to be a premier event for professionals and enthusiasts alike, fostering innovation and collaboration in the tech community.

Our Mission

At TechCon 2024, our mission is to connect technology professionals, enthusiasts, and innovators from around the world. We aim to provide a platform for sharing knowledge, exploring new ideas, and fostering collaboration within the tech community.

We are committed to promoting diversity and inclusion in technology, ensuring that voices from all backgrounds are heard and valued.
